Career Track is a web platform that provides with a guide on the choices that you make hile pursuing your career goals in Uganda. It provides a space where you can view recommended subject combinations at UACE level, recommended course application choices for gorvernment and private scheme at different public universities in Uganda hence providing you with a better chance of achieving your desired career goals.

You can also read different articles in the blog and read about different people's views on achievemenet, education and careers. Take a tour on the platform and change your life. Thanks!!!!!!
